Semester Ticket Information about Semesterticket

The Rhein-Neckar Region has an excellent local public transportation system, which students can avail inexpensively using the semester ticket. This ticket allows students to travel for free within the Rhein-Neckar Region.

When:

After enrollment.

Where:

Ground Floor, Maria-Probst-Strasse 3 | Main Train Station (Hauptbahnhof) | Online on DB Website

Cost:

EUR 170

Validity:

6 Months (1 Semester)
