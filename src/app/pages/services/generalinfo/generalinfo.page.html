General Information University Information

In one of Germany's most beautiful cities, we think and live a new university. Our innovative study model based on the CORE principle goes beyond mere knowledge transfer, because we focus on competence-oriented learning. We consistently align learning objectives, teaching and forms of examination with later professional life, so that you can acquire exactly those professional, methodological and social skills that are needed on the job market today.
