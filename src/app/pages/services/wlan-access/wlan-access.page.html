Wlan Access WLAN Access Information


To access the Eduroam Network and University WLAN, you must be enrolled and have you matriculation number at hand. To connect, follow the given steps:

  1. Search for ‘Eduroam’ or ‘wlanfh1x’ network and select either of them.

  2. Enter your username and password:

    3. Username: your_matriculation_number@stud.hochschule-heidelberg.de

    Password: The password associated with your SRH HSHD Account.

  3. Click on “Connect” and you can access the internet.


NOTE: You can access the Eduroam network in any University campus, all over Europe.
